The Story

On 30/11/1987, a four-man PRA scouting team suffered a structural reality failure in Level 0.1. Three researchers fell; two were confirmed deceased and utilized as biological decoys by Entity 0.1-A (The Mimic Scorpion). The third, Noah Evans, survived by severing his suit's RBS beacon, effectively "deleting" his presence from PRA tracking systems.

Evans' recovered VHS logs detail a four-month odyssey through non-Euclidean environments. After escaping Level 0.1, Evans entered Level 2 (The Meadows), surviving until 12/04/1988. Upon discovering a primitive interface, Evans successfully executed a jmp command to Level 0x74B1B1. Within this "Machine Code" sector, he encountered six individuals from 1984.

Following a transit through Level 4 (The Poolrooms) and a spiritual encounter in Level 3 (The Pink House), Evans bypassed a "Frontrooms Window" in Level 5, choosing instead an erroneously labeled "EXIT" door. This returned Evans to Level 0 on 14/04/1988. Due to extreme physical de-sync (Subject was recorded hovering 2 inches above floor plane), the guarding RRT unit identified Evans as a Class-V Mimic. Subject escaped RRT team, and got to Base Camp Sector East, where he now is in the quarentine. Has been confirmed he is not a mimic.

Final Result: Access to Level 0.1 sealed with 250 tons of concrete. Budget for Project "Vault-Keeper" redirected to quarantine maintenance. Noah Evans remains a "Ghost File"—his family was informed he died in a chemical spill at a MoD site in Suffolk.

Mathematical Mechanics

The rate of mental degradation is calculated by the deviation from an individual's "Average Luminosity" (D).

SD = L / (T + (2.4536 x D))
  • SD: Sanity Decrease
  • L: Luminosity of current environment
  • T: Time spent in current luminosity
  • D: Distance from average luminosity (user baseline)

When SD reaches the SLL (Sanity Limit Low), absorption begins.

What is absorption?

Absorption happens when a person reaches their SLL(Sanity Limit Low). Not the same as subsorption(Reach SLH).

When the process begins, the person starts to lose all memories and cognitive abilities, similar to having dementia.

Shortly after that, they begin to dislike light and all sounds that aren't their own footsteps.

Finally, the person completes their absorption with either:

    Becoming a dark blob roaming.

    Becoming another silhouette in the frontrooms.

Can I save people in the process of absorption?

Short answer: Yes, but its really hard.

To save someone from absorption, you'd need to put them in a really well-lit room, where luminosity > 80, give them almond water for around 50 Licles straight.

Frontrooms Manifestations

The entity leaves "markers" or "lures" to attract potential mass.

  • The Slide: A closed slide appearing in domestic basements.
  • The Door A door that tempts you to enter it. Dissapears behind you as soon as closed.
  • Silhouettes: Figures seen at midnight; "blink and you miss it."
  • The Polaroid: Photographs of current rooms featuring non-existent objects.
